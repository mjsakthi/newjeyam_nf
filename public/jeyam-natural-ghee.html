Jeyam Natural Ghee

Jeyam Natural Ghee Jayam Natural Ghee is made with 100% organic butter from grass-fed cows on farms, the cow's milk, which can only be obtained from whole cows, is simply creamed and then butter is extracted from it to get its shelf life.

High temperature boiling results in higher quantity but lower quality. As we boil at low temperature only less quantity is obtained and the moisture level in it decreases and its quality increases. This gives a quality, clean and natural taste.

Prepared by homemade style without any technical assistance, our Jayam Natural Ghee is used for medicinal purposes in more than 25 hospitals.
